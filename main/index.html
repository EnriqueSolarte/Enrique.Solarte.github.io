Bolivar Enrique Solarte Pardo (宋子宣 - Kike)

enrique.solarte.pardo[at]gmail.com

I am a PhD. candidate at National Tsing Hua University, under the advisory of Prof. Min Sun in the Vision Science Lab (VSLab) since 2018.

My comprehensive knowledge covers mechanical engineering topics, such as CNC machines and CAD/CAM Systems; electrical & electronic engineering, such as Control Applications and edge-device applications focus on computer vision. My PhD. research focuses on Machine Learning and Deep Learning applications for visual geometry perception based on multi-view cameras.
